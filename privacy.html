Privacy

Updated July 2026

PayoutAudit is a free, advertising-supported discovery page. It is built to work without collecting personal information from you.

What this page collects

  • Nothing you type. There are no accounts, no forms, and no sign-up on this site. Your quiz answers are used on your device to sort the offers and are not transmitted or stored by us.
  • Ad campaign data. If you arrive from an ad (for example on TikTok), the ad platform may append campaign parameters, such as a click ID, to the link. Those parameters pass through to the partner link when you tap an offer so the partner can credit the visit. We do not use them to identify you.
  • Standard server logs. Our hosting provider keeps routine technical logs (such as IP address and request time) to run and secure the service, as virtually all websites do.

What this page does not do

  • No cookies set by us, and no local storage.
  • No sale of personal information.
  • No email lists. We never ask for your email on this site.

When you leave this page

Tapping an offer takes you to a partner site with its own privacy policy and its own data practices. Anything you provide there, such as an email address, is governed by that partner, not by us. Read their policy before signing up.
